Privacy and Policy

Privacy Policy for TaskBot Effective Date: 25/10/2024
At TaskBot, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting the information of users interacting with the bot on Discord. This policy outlines the types of information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

TaskBot collects limited information necessary to provide its functionality:

  • User ID: Used to respond to commands and ensure personalized features work correctly.

  • Server ID: Collected to store server-specific settings and configurations.

  • Command Inputs: The text of commands issued to TaskBot is stored temporarily for processing the requested action.

  • Other Metadata: Information such as timestamps and usage statistics may be collected for performance monitoring and improving features.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Respond to and process commands and interactions.

  • Provide server-specific settings and maintain configuration across sessions.

  • Monitor and optimize bot performance.

Data Storage and Retention

TaskBot stores data only as long as it is necessary for the bot to function properly. User and server data will be automatically deleted when TaskBot is removed from the server, or upon specific request.

Data Sharing and Security

We do not share, sell, or disclose your information to third parties, except where required by law or to protect our users. All data is stored securely, and access is restricted to authorized individuals responsible for maintaining the bot.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our services or data practices. Any updates will be announced in the bot's support server or via direct communication.
